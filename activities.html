TSV ACTIVITIES

TSV TALK AND WORKSHOP

The TSV Talk and Workshop held in Legazpi City, Albay was actively attended by TSV officers from the Ecclesiastical District of Catanduanes. This activity provided valuable support in enhancing their technical knowledge, particularly in areas such as cable management. The workshop emphasized the importance of proper cable organization, which plays a vital role in maintaining the functionality and longevity of equipment within the TSV room. Through this initiative, participants gained practical insights and skills that will help improve the overall efficiency and upkeep of their technical setups.

DISTRICT OF CATANDUANES EARNED 3rd PLACE

The District of Catanduanes earned 3rd Place Overall in the various activities conducted during the workshop. This recognition reflects the dedication and growing competence of the Catanduanes TSV officers in fulfilling their technical duties. The award brought great joy to those who participated in the event and served as a strong motivation for them to continue performing their duties with even more enthusiasm and commitment.
